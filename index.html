A House. A Kitchen.
A Legacy.

N Veedu started as a family home on Mettupalayam Road. Today, it's Coimbatore's warmest dining destination — where generations gather, friendships are celebrated, and every dish carries the soul of Tamil home-cooking.

Walk in as a guest, leave as family. Our home serves the finest North and South Indian delicacies, prepared using grandmother's secret spice blends and locally sourced ingredients.
